# Dropcut

## Overview
Video editing is time consuming. It takes a content creator 50 minutes on average to edit and produce a video. If you had the option to have the video be edited for you, wouldn’t you rather save that time from editing and spend it on strategy and growth instead?

Introducing Dropcut, a mobile app that helps users edit and produce upload-ready short form videos in minutes. Whether you are a content creator, business owner, or avid social media user, you can simply upload videos from your camera roll and have Dropcut impress your audience with its editing.

Download today to experience effortless editing. Happy creating!

---

## Motivation

We conducted user research to understand existing solutions and pain points for video editing. We surveyed 13 content creators who work on different content niches (food, fitness, fashion, travel, lifestyle). Most of them reported that visiting businesses and trying products is their favorite part about content creation, rather than editing videos. The survey participants also reported that choosing the best clips and ordering the clips are their least favorite parts of video editing. Dropcut was built to eliminate that friction, so creators can spend more time on the work they actually enjoy.

---

## Features

### Customize Editing Style
- Select your content category (food, fashion, travel, etc.)
- Choose your target video length
- Provide freeform editing instructions (e.g. *"start with an overhead shot, focus on food items, use steady movements"*)
- Select overlay text fonts

### Upload & Edit
- Import unedited raw footage directly from your camera roll
- Rearrange clip order in the Dropcut-generated edit
- Adjust individual clip lengths
- Add and modify overlay text captions

### Export & Publish
- Download the finished video to your camera roll
- Share directly to Instagram or TikTok

### Project Management
- Return to and continue editing previous projects
